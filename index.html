How to Use Voice to Text

1
Start Recording

Press Ctrl+Shift+R or click the record button. Make sure your microphone is working.

2
Speak Clearly

Talk normally into your microphone. You'll see a waveform visualization showing your voice input.

3
Finish Recording

Press Ctrl+Shift+R again or click the stop button to end recording.

4
Text Ready!

Your speech is automatically transcribed and saved to clipboard. Just press Ctrl+V to paste anywhere!

Enable "Local WhisperX Mode" in settings to run offline, or add an OpenAI API key
