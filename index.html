Tips

• App runs in the background when closed - check your system tray

• Right-click the recording pill for quick actions

• Use global shortcuts even when app is minimized

• Transcribed text is automatically copied to clipboard for pasting

How to Use Voice to Text

1
Start Recording

Press Cmd+Shift+R or click the record button. Make sure your microphone is working.

2
Speak Clearly

Talk normally into your microphone. You'll see a waveform visualization showing your voice input.

3
Finish Recording

Press Cmd+Shift+R again or click the stop button to end recording.

4
Text Ready!

Your speech is automatically transcribed and saved to clipboard. Just press Cmd+V to paste anywhere!

Configure your OpenAI API key in settings to enable transcription
